# Privacy Policy

**MindMaster – AI Learning & Memorization Coach**

*Last updated: June 2026*

---

## 1. Introduction

Welcome to MindMaster ("we", "our", or "us"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application ("App"). Please read this policy carefully. If you disagree with its terms, please discontinue use of the App.

---

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Information You Provide Directly

* **Account Information:** When you register, we collect your email address and a hashed password.
* **Content You Create:** Study topics, quiz subjects, project names, and any text you input into the App to generate learning content.
* **Communication:** If you contact us for support, we may keep records of that correspondence.

### 2.2 Information Collected Automatically

* **Usage Data:** How you interact with the App, including screens visited, features used, quiz scores, streaks, and learning progress.
* **Device Information:** Device type, operating system version, and crash/error logs.
* **Token & Subscription Status:** Your in-app token balance, subscription tier (Free, Premium), and purchase history.

### 2.3 Information We Do NOT Collect

* We do not collect your precise location.
* We do not access your contacts, photos, or microphone without explicit permission.
* We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

---

## 3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

* Create and manage your account.
* Generate personalized AI-powered study content and quizzes.
* Track your learning progress, streaks, and mastery scores.
* Process in-app purchases and manage subscription entitlements.
* Send you study reminders and important notifications only if you opt in.
* Improve the App's features, performance, and content quality.
* Respond to your support requests.
* Comply with applicable legal obligations.

---

## 4. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information. We may share data with:

### 4.1 Service Providers

* **AI Content Providers, including OpenAI:** Your study topic inputs may be sent to OpenAI's API and may be processed by other third-party AI providers to generate quiz questions, study plans, summaries, memory cards, and other learning content. OpenAI processes this data under their own Privacy Policy. Other AI providers operate under their own policies. We do not send your name or email address to AI providers. AI-generated content may contain inaccuracies, omissions, or hallucinations.
* **Expo / Expo Push Notifications:** We use Expo's notification infrastructure to deliver optional study reminders.
* **RevenueCat:** We use RevenueCat for subscription and token purchase management.
* **Apple App Store / Google Play Store:** Payment processing for in-app purchases is handled by Apple and Google. We do not receive your full payment card details.

### 4.2 Legal Requirements

We may disclose information if required by law, court order, or governmental authority.

---

## 5. Data Retention

* Your account and associated data are retained as long as your account is active.
* You can permanently delete all your data at any time via **Settings → Danger Zone → Erase All My Data**.
* After deletion, your data is removed from our active databases within 30 days.

---

## 6. Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including:

* Bcrypt password hashing. Passwords are never stored in plain text.
* HTTPS/TLS encryption for data in transit.
* Access controls limiting who can view user data internally.

However, no method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure.

---

## 7. Children's Privacy

MindMaster is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to:

* **Access** the personal data we hold about you.
* **Correct** inaccurate data.
* **Delete** your account and all associated data via the in-app Erase Data feature.
* **Opt out** of non-essential notifications at any time via Settings.
* **Data portability**: request a copy of your data in a machine-readable format.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at the email below.
